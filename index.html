FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Have questions? We've got answers.

What are the age categories? +

We have two main categories: "For Kids" (ages 5-12) and "For Adults" (ages 13 and up). Please ensure you enter the correct category based on your age at the time of submission.

What file formats are accepted for submission? +

We accept video files (MP4, MOV) and high-quality audio files (MP3). While video is preferred, a great audio performance is also highly valued. The maximum file size is 200MB.
